Benefits of Membership

Charitable Giving to Student Success

The Faculty Association provides an opportunity to directly and immediately help Maricopa students in need through the Maricopa Colleges Faculty Foundation.

Shared Governance

The Faculty Association actively promotes shared governance and faculty leadership participates in formulating policies, procedures, and allocating resources at the college and district levels.

Academic Freedom and Professional Standards 

  Academic freedom is under relentless attack by special interest groups, judicial decisions, and legislative initiatives.  The Faculty Association maintains constant vigilance on these and related matters.  It also offers educational workshops on shared governance, academic freedom, peer review, and other professional values and standards.

Meet and Confer 

The Meet and Confer Team works tirelessly to negotiate with the Governing Board to improve salary, benefits, and working conditions for all Residential Faculty. Faculty working conditions are student learning conditions.  Only Faculty Association members are eligible to vote on proposed changes to the RFP.

Meet and Confer Voting

While all Residential faculty benefit from the RFP, only Faculty Association Members are allowed to vote to ratify the annual M&C ballot on changes to the RFP. Make sure your voice counts!

Legal Assistance

The Faculty Association retains the services of Mike Napier of the Law Offices of Napier, Coury & Baillie, PC to help protect the employment rights of members. Mike has been with the FA for over 35 years and knows us and our contract better than anyone.

Grievance / Controversy Representation 

The Faculty Association supports faculty involved in grievance and resolution of controversy processes.

Political Action

FacPAC is the only political action committee in Arizona composed of higher education faculty. Through voluntary contributions to the Faculty PAC, in recent years we have made a decisive difference in electing governing board members and legislators who support students and understand the faculty’s role in achieving student success.

Lobbying

The Faculty Association retains Mike Bielecki of the law firm of Lewis & Roca, LLP to advocate for the interests of our members in legislative, governmental, and other fora. Mike has been essential to our efforts in fighting the Guns on Campus legislation.

Free Wills, Living Wills & Healthcare Power of Attorney 

Association members, spouses, and dependent children are entitled to a free Will, Living Will, and Healthcare Power of Attorney.

Free One-Hour Consultation on any Legal Matter

Association members, spouses, and dependent children are entitled to a free one-hour consultation with an attorney on any legal matter.

Personal Injury/Wrongful Death

Association members are entitled to a reduced contingency fee of 25%. Additionally anyone referred by an FA member will receive the same discount.

Referrals to Attorneys for other Legal Matters

Association members will be provided with a referral to a qualified attorney at discounted rates for matters such as bankruptcy, family law, criminal defense, and estate planning.

Supplemental Non-MCCCD Legal Benefits for FA Members
We strive to respond to requests within 48 hours. If you do not receive a response, please email personnel@mccfa.org.
