RFP Faculty Dues

As the District-wide Faculty professional membership association for Maricopa Community Colleges, the Maricopa Community Colleges Faculty Association receives its strength through the power of the faculty who join and remain members. For decades, the Faculty Association has provided focused representation for Residential Faculty to promote shared governance, academic freedom, and member benefits.

With members at all ten campuses across the District, the Faculty Association continues to strengthen the position of Faculty with College and District administration, the Governing Board, and the State legislature.

Your Senators, Faculty Executive Council representatives, and Association Officers are dedicated to representing the interests of Residential Faculty across our unique colleges, and we welcome your membership and voice through various means of participation in the Association.
