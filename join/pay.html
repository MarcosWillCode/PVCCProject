RFP Faculty Dues

As the District-wide Faculty professional membership association for Maricopa Community Colleges, the Maricopa Community Colleges Faculty Association receives its strength through the power of the faculty who join and remain members. For decades, the Faculty Association has provided focused representation for Residential Faculty to promote shared governance, academic freedom, and member benefits.

With members at all ten campuses across the District, the Faculty Association continues to strengthen the position of Faculty with College and District administration, the Governing Board, and the State legislature.

Your Senators, Faculty Executive Council representatives, and Association Officers are dedicated to representing the interests of Residential Faculty across our unique colleges and we welcome your membership and voice through various means of participation in the Association.

Stand up with your colleagues and join today!

Membership Dues Payment Options

ACH Direct Debit

The electronic form allows you to choose between a one+time annual payment, the 4-pay payment plan, or monthly payments through direct debits to your checking account. These payments will be withdrawn from your account per the cycle you choose until you notify us to stop. Set it once and forget it!

Note: For those currently on PayPal who want to switch over, please remember to stop your PayPal recurring payments. We will adjust your monthly ACH payments based on your previous PayPal payments already made.

Questions?

For payment questions contact Megan McGuire at finance@mccfa.org

FacPAC and Foundation

To make a one-time payment or to set up recurring payments to the FacPAC or the Foundation, please visit their webpages.

Payment options

Annual Payment - Credit Card

Your credit card will be charged one time for the annual membership dues total.

Save $15 by paying in full before September 1st!

4-Pay - Credit Card

Your credit card will automatically be charged four times (Today, then again in October, January, and April during the first week of the month).

Monthly - Credit Card

Once you sign up and make your first payment, your credit card will automatically be charged 11 more times (approximately one month between charges during the first week of the month).

Note: For those currently on PayPal who want to switch over, please remember to stop your PayPal recurring payments. We will adjust your monthly ACH payments based on your previous PayPal payments already made.
