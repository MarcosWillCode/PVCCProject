Faculty Association Officer

President

Robert Soza

Hi, I’m Robert Soza; the President for the Faculty Association. I am English Faculty at Mesa Community College. I am in my eighth year as a full-time faculty member in Maricopa.

My role as President is to support the Association. I am genuinely proud to be part of such a remarkable team here at the Association; seeing how thoughtfully everyone works to support faculty interests is impressive. Also, it would be rude of me to not also acknowledge the tremendous faculty who work with me at MCC. I am fortunate to be a part of two great teams.

President - Elect

Mike Mitchell

I am Mike Mitchell, the Faculty Association President-elect. Paradise Valley Community College is my home college where I teach in the English Division. I have been a full-time faculty member of the MCCCD for 25 years.

As President-elect, my primary role is to support the work of President Robert Soza and Past-President Salina Bednarek. Secondarily, it is my role to learn the many functions of the FA President and to orient myself to the operations, projects and goals of the Faculty Association in preparation for serving as its President in 17-18. Today, we have the opportunity to provide shape and direction to the future of the MCCCD in ways previous generations of faculty could not have imagined. It is my belief that we can and will come together to do this in the coming year.

Past President

Salina Bednarek

My name is Salina Bednarek and I am MCCFA Past President. My home college is Phoenix College where I am Nursing faculty and currently serving as Department Chair. I am in my ninth year as Residential Faculty and have been fortunate to serve in a number of college and district leadership capacities. 

As the FA Past President, I will continue to support and advocate for the rights of our members and all faculty of Maricopa as we navigate through unprecedented times. My commitment is support FA President Robert Soza, serve on the Meet & Confer Team and to serve on various committees and task forces as assigned. I am excited to be a part of the FA Leadership team and look forward to a great year.

Personnel Officer

Patty flinkenstadt

My name is Patty Finkenstadt, and I am serving as the Faculty Association’s Personnel Officer. I have been a Residential Faculty in the Biosciences Department at Phoenix College for 10 years, and have served in a variety of leadership roles almost from Day One.

As Personnel Officer, my role is multi-fold. I provide training and advice to members of FEC and to the faculty at the Colleges; I help faculty understand their rights and responsibilities as described in the RFP; and I provide guidance in seeking resolution if someone feels a violation of their rights has occurred.
