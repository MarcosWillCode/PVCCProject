Mission

The Faculty Association Political Action Committee supports higher education in Arizona through political engagement.

(See the FAQs below in links)

Philosophy

Non-Partisan, Non-Idealogoical, focusing on candidates, legislation and issues that affect MCCCD faculty and students.

Contributions to the Maricopa Community Colleges Faculty PAC are not tax deductible. Contributions are optional and will be used for political purposes.  
The State of Arizona requires us to report the name, address, occupation, and employer of any person whose aggregate contributions exceed $50 during an election cycle.
