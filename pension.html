Protect Your Pension

Public Pensions are under attack!
Well funded and organized groups around the country are seeking to shut down public pensions like the Arizona State Retirement System. These groups want to eliminate defined benefit plans like ours and replace them with high-risk defined contribution (401k style) plans. The retirement you’ve worked for, that you’ve earned is being threatened right now! Help us PROTECT OUR PENSIONS!
